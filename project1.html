Automated Liver Tissue Analysis Pipeline

Created a computer vision-AI pipeline to automate liver tissue analysis and quantify steatosis in donor liver images, reducing latency by 100x. The pipeline consists of Python-based image preprocessing, a CVAI model, GPU-accelerated processing on a Jetson board scaled with Google Cloud Functions, and is integrated with an iOS application (currently under legal review for distribution).
