TreeHacks 2025: Custom-Fitted Insole iOS App

iOS LiDAR 3D Printing Healthcare

Developed an innovative iOS app that leverages LiDAR to scan a user's feet and generate a custom-fitted, 3D-printable insole in just 2 minutes. Using an iPhone and a $50 3D printer, this solution provides an affordable and rapid way to produce personalized shoe insoles.

Project Overview

This project was created for TreeHacks 2025 to tackle the challenge of obtaining well-fitting, custom insoles quickly and inexpensively. By capturing detailed foot measurements using LiDAR technology, the app processes the data to produce a 3D model of an insole that can be directly 3D printed.

Key Features

  • LiDAR Integration: Utilizes iPhone's LiDAR sensor for precise and detailed foot scanning.
  • 3D Model Generation: Advanced algorithms convert scan data into printable insole designs.
  • Rapid Processing: Complete workflow from scan to model in just 2 minutes.
  • Cost-Effective Solution: Compatible with affordable 3D printers, making custom insoles accessible.
